- Participation is initially restricted to students of ML course 2022 - University of Pisa.
All participants with their submission agree to the disclosure and publication of their name, results with preliminary and final ranking. 


- Delivery rules and deadlines: see slide of ML-2022 PRJ Lecture:
The Material (that include a package* as specified in the slides of ML-2022 Lecture for the project)  must be provided through 
the e-learning platform for the ML course, till the deadline specified in each folder of the Moodle site 
(see the folder for each session in the e-learning platform ).
An email to announce the upload with the tag [ML-2022] must be sent to micheli@di.unipi.it and to the course assistant, 
till the same deadline.
 
(*) The package tipically include: your code (if original, else use a link to libraries in the report), 
your written report (with results on 3 MONKs and the CUP), team-name_ML-CUP22-TS.csv, team-name_abstract.txt
Deliver the package only as zip file!!!
Only 1 delivery for each group, by 1 member of the group.
Use the surnames of the group components in the package file name. 
And in the pdf report file.
